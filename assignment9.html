HOLOPORTATION:Virtual 3D Teleportation

November 09 2018

Holoportation is a new immersive telepresence system developed by Microsoft.It combines the ability to capture high quality 3D models of people, objects and environments in real-time.It is enabled into action with the help of virtual or augmented reality displays.The innovative technology provides the ability to see, hear and interact with participants located far away by virtually teleporting.

This technology brings collaboration to complete new level.The enhanced form of interaction among users is what we imagined in future is now reality.Existence in same space despite being located remote makes more sense and immersion.The difference from previously introduced technology is the unrestricted motion of user in space that is made possible with current available hardware.The quality of performance is another point to notice here that is again flawless live streaming.The interaction with other 3d objects is another amazing feature.One of the striking feature that I liked specially is that now it is also possible in moving environment.For this the bandwidth required is 30-50 Mbps and works well till the user is in the wifi range.The image below describes the implementation of this technology.
