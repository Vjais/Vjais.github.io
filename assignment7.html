IKEA PLACE Review

This week I had a chance to test another augmented reality application.It is the IKEA PLACE.This application allows the user to experiment with furniture through the app by placing it at any place they prefer.The user just have to install the application and select the furniture of their choice from the list.After choosing the furniture,the application access the camera and places the furniture in the desired surrounding.The more clear explaination is in the images below.The first image I took at library by placing a sofa set which kind of fits in the scenario.The next picture I took at home by placing a table in the hall.
